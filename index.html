Founder & CEO, Rose-fashion-accessories

ROSE FASHION is the visionary founder and CEO of Rose-fashion-accessories — Lagos' most trusted premium lifestyle store. With a deep passion for fashion, elegance, and African excellence, she built Rose-fashion-accessories from the ground up with one mission: to make luxury accessible to every African.

Since 2019, ROSE has curated hundreds of premium jewelry pieces, designer bags, luxury watches, and statement shades — handpicking every single item to ensure her clients receive nothing but the best. Her attention to detail, commitment to authenticity, and warm customer service have earned Rose-fashion-accessories a loyal community of 1,000+ satisfied clients across Nigeria.
